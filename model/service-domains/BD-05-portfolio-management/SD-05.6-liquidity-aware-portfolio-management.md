# SD-05.6 — Liquidity-Aware Portfolio Management

**Business Domain:** BD-05 Portfolio Management (Front office) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Manages a portfolio's liquidity profile so its obligations can be met without forced sales. Where SD-01.11 Liquidity Strategy & Tiering sets the institution's total-portfolio liquidity policy and SD-07.3 Liquidity Risk Management produces the per-holding liquidity classification, this Service Domain applies them within a portfolio: it consumes the classification, sizes the liquid buffer, models the funding capacity for unfunded commitments, and stress-tests the portfolio against redemption and capital-call scenarios.

## Service Operations

- **Build the portfolio liquidity budget** — set the liquidity the portfolio must hold against its expected and stressed obligations, against the SD-07.3 per-holding liquidity classification.
- **Manage unfunded-commitment funding capacity** — track the unfunded-commitment schedule and maintain the capital-call coverage the private-markets sleeve requires.
- **Size and maintain the liquidity sleeve** — set and run the short-duration liquid buffer that absorbs redemption and call shocks.
- **Run liquidity stress testing** — test the portfolio's liquidity against redemption, capital-call and market-stress scenarios.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the SD-07.3 per-holding liquidity classification; the holdings with their liquidity attributes; the SD-01.11 liquidity-tier taxonomy and buffer policy; the unfunded-commitment schedule and the SD-09.7 cash-flow forecast; the redemption and spending forecast.
- **Outputs:** the liquidity budget, the coverage ratios and the stress-test results — feeding SD-05.3 (rebalancing for liquidity) and BD-11.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Portfolio / Mandate (E-03), Holding / Position (E-04, `book = ibor`), Valuation (E-07, any `method`), Risk Measurement (E-19, `risk_type = liquidity`) for the SD-07.3 per-holding liquidity classification; LP Commitment (PM-06) and Fund Investment (PM-09) for the unfunded-commitment funding demand; the SD-01.11 liquidity-tier taxonomy and buffer policy; the SD-07.3 per-holding liquidity classification; the unfunded-commitment schedule and the SD-09.7 cash-flow forecast.
- **Owns:** none — the liquidity budget is an analytical artefact; the liquidity classification is SD-07.3's (consumed here, not produced).

## Standards

- No single external standard governs liquidity-aware portfolio management. The liquidity-budget and coverage-ratio disciplines are the practitioner conventions; it applies the SD-01.11 total-portfolio liquidity strategy against the SD-07.3 per-holding liquidity classification.

## Open extensions

- The boundary with SD-01.11 (tier taxonomy and placement rules), SD-07.3 (produces the per-holding liquidity classification and measures liquidity risk) and SD-11.2 (treasury funding) is recorded in the BD-01 README — SD-05.6 consumes the classification, it does not produce it.
