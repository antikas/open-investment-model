# SD-03.1 — Manager Sourcing & Pipeline

**Business Domain:** BD-03 Manager & Fund Investment (Front office) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Identifies and maintains a pipeline of external managers and funds for potential allocation. Manager Sourcing & Pipeline is the front of the fund-investing chain: it turns the whole population of external managers into a managed, stage-gated pipeline of candidates worth researching. It sources both ways an institution finds managers — through its own networks and through the consultant gatekeepers — and screens for first-pass fit. It produces candidates; it does not research or select them.

## Service Operations

- **Maintain the manager universe** — the database of external managers and funds the institution tracks, by strategy, asset class and vehicle type.
- **Source managers** — originate candidates through networks, conferences, placement agents, consultant pipelines and emerging-manager scanning.
- **Screen for initial fit** — first-pass filtering on strategy, asset class, vehicle, size and capacity against the allocation need.
- **Maintain and stage-gate the pipeline** — track candidates through the stages from sourced to longlisted, with the re-up calendar for existing managers.
- **Commission or consume external manager research** — engage an investment consultant for a manager search, or consume a consultant's standing manager-research ratings.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the allocation need from BD-01; manager databases and the consultant manager-research universe; networks, conferences and placement-agent contact.
- **Outputs:** a screened manager pipeline and longlist — consumed by **SD-03.2 Manager Research & Selection** (which researches the candidates) and by SD-03.8's re-up calendar.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Legal Entity (E-01) — the external manager and GP as roles of the party master; Fund & Vehicle (PM-01).
- **Owns:** none — the pipeline is an analytical artefact.

## Standards

- No external standard governs sourcing. The investment-consultant manager-research industry (Mercer, Cambridge Associates, Albourne, bfinance, Aon) is the de-facto external manager universe many institutions source through.
