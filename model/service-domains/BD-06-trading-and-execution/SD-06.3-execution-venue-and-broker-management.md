# SD-06.3 — Execution Venue & Broker Management

**Business Domain:** BD-06 Trading & Execution (Front office) · **Applies:** PUB

## Purpose

Manages the universe of counterparties and venues the desk can trade with — who the firm may trade with, and where. Execution Venue & Broker Management maintains the approved-broker and venue lists, monitors the counterparties' trading risk, and keeps the connectivity that lets the desk reach the market. It defines the choices SD-06.1's routing and SD-06.2's execution draw on.

## Service Operations

- **Maintain the approved broker and dealer list** — onboard brokers and dealers, run their due diligence, and periodically review them.
- **Maintain the execution-venue list** — onboard and classify the venues the desk routes to.
- **Monitor counterparty trading risk** — track counterparty credit and the trading lines against them.
- **Manage trading-commission arrangements** — the commission rates and commission-sharing arrangements with brokers.
- **Produce broker and venue scorecards** — the performance scorecards, fed by the execution-quality data from SD-06.4.
- **Manage connectivity** — the FIX sessions and venue and platform onboarding.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** counterparty due-diligence data; the execution-quality data from SD-06.4; market-structure information.
- **Outputs:** the approved broker and venue lists, the commission arrangements and the connectivity — consumed by **SD-06.1** (routing) and **SD-06.2** (execution).

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Legal Entity (E-01) — brokers, dealers and venues as roles of the party master; the SD-06.4 execution-quality data.
- **Owns:** none — the approved lists are operating-model artefacts.

## Standards

- The MiFID II venue taxonomy — regulated market, multilateral trading facility, organised trading facility, systematic internaliser — and, for the US, exchanges, alternative trading systems and swap-execution facilities. The venue taxonomy spans equity, fixed-income and FX venues, not equity exchanges alone.

## Open extensions

- The boundary with SD-02.8 — SD-06.3 manages brokers for *execution*; SD-02.8 manages and pays providers for *research*, and commission-sharing arrangements straddle the two.
- The boundary with SD-17.8 Vendor, Outsourcing & Service-Provider Oversight on the commercial relationship.
