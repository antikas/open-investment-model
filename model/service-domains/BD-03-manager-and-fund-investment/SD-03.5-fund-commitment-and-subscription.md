# SD-03.5 — Fund Commitment & Subscription

**Business Domain:** BD-03 Manager & Fund Investment (Front office) · **Applies:** PRIV

## Purpose

Executes the legal commitment to a closed-end fund and manages the subscription process. Fund Commitment & Subscription is one of the two execution paths of BD-03 — the private-markets path, where the institution becomes a limited partner in a fund with a capital-call lifecycle, a fixed term and a J-curve. It turns an **approved-by-the-IC** selection into a legal LP interest: negotiating the agreement, securing the institution's bespoke terms, completing the subscription, and joining the fund at a closing. SD-03.5 cannot start a subscription without the Investment Committee authorisation from **SD-03.9 Fund-Commitment Approval & Authorisation** — the IC gate is the trigger that releases the legal close to proceed.

## Service Operations

- **Negotiate and review the LPA** — the limited partnership agreement: economics, governance, key-person and investment-period terms.
- **Negotiate the side letter and co-investment rights** — the institution's bespoke terms — fee arrangements, reporting, excuse rights — and the co-investment rights that originate later BD-04 deal flow.
- **Complete the subscription** — the subscription documents, AML / KYC and investor-eligibility certification.
- **Participate in fund closings** — join at the first, an interim, or the final close, with the equalisation payment due for a later close.
- **Run the most-favoured-nation election** — after final close, review the disclosed universe of other LPs' side-letter terms and elect the more favourable ones the institution is entitled to.
- **Set up the capital account** — establish the institution's capital account and undrawn-commitment balance against which calls are drawn.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the **SD-03.9 IC authorisation** (the trigger that lets the legal close proceed); the selection recommendation (SD-03.2) and investment due-diligence report (SD-03.4); the commitment-pacing budget from SD-01.10; the fund's LPA and subscription documents.
- **Outputs:** an executed LP Commitment — consumed by **BD-12 SD-12.8** (which processes the capital calls and distributions against it), **SD-03.6** (which monitors the manager) and **SD-09.7** (which forecasts its cash flows).

## Entities

- **Consumes:** the SD-03.9 IC authorisation (the gate that releases the legal close); Fund & Vehicle (PM-01), Fund Terms (PM-10); the SD-01.10 pacing budget; the SD-03.2 selection recommendation; the SD-03.4 investment due-diligence report.
- **Owns:** **LP Commitment (PM-06)** — the institution's committed and undrawn capital in a fund, and the side-letter terms attached to it. SD-03.5 is its authoritative source.

## Standards

- **ILPA Principles 3.0** (2019) and the **ILPA Model Limited Partnership Agreement** (with the **ILPA Model Subscription Agreement** and **ILPA Model NDA**) for the LPA and side-letter documentation. The most-favoured-nation election is the industry-standard mechanism by which side-letter terms are equalised across LPs.

## Open extensions

- A Side Letter entity — the bespoke per-LP terms are a managed obligation set, consumed downstream by SD-10.5 Side-Letter & Fund-Term Compliance.
- The boundary with SD-03.7 — SD-03.5 is the closed-end fund-commitment path; SD-03.7 is the open-ended segregated-mandate path.
