# SD-05.2 — Portfolio Management & Monitoring

**Business Domain:** BD-05 Portfolio Management (Front office) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Runs the ongoing oversight of a portfolio against its objectives, benchmark and constraints — the feedback step of the portfolio-management process. Where SD-05.1 builds the target portfolio, this Service Domain watches the live one: it detects when the portfolio has drifted from target, when the investor's circumstances or the market have changed enough to matter, and when a risk limit is approached — and it fires the triggers that the rebalancing, overlay and transition domains act on.

## Service Operations

- **Monitor portfolio drift** — track the live holdings against the target portfolio and the policy bands.
- **Monitor against mandate and benchmark** — confirm the portfolio still conforms to the mandate and track performance against the benchmark (consuming BD-09's measurement).
- **Monitor ex-ante risk** — track tracking error, value-at-risk, factor exposures and concentration against the risk limits.
- **Monitor changes in circumstance and market** — investor-circumstance changes and shifts in capital-market conditions that warrant a portfolio review.
- **Generate exceptions and triggers** — raise the drift, risk and review exceptions and fire the triggers consumed by SD-05.3, SD-05.4 and SD-05.8.
- **Run the portfolio review cycle** — the periodic portfolio review and its reporting to the investment committee.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the live portfolio and prices; the target portfolio (SD-05.1); the mandate and risk limits; the benchmark; updated capital-market expectations.
- **Outputs:** drift and exception reports, risk dashboards, and the rebalance / adjustment triggers — consumed by **SD-05.3**, **SD-05.4**, **SD-05.8** and the investment committee.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Holding / Position (E-04, `book = ibor`), Valuation (E-07, any `method`), Benchmark / Index (E-10), Risk Limit (E-16), Risk Measurement (E-19, any `risk_type`); the mandate facet of E-03 from SD-01.2; the SD-05.1 target portfolio.
- **Owns:** the **portfolio facet** of **E-03 Portfolio / Mandate** — the live holdings container, the constraint-monitoring subject, the report subject. SD-05.2 is the ongoing authoritative source for the portfolio's operative state; the mandate facet (objectives, return target, risk appetite, constraints) is owned by **SD-01.2 Investment Mandate & Policy Definition** under the faceted-ownership pattern recorded in [`ownership-map.md`](../../ownership-map.md). E-03 is the model's only faceted entity.

## Standards

- The **CFA Institute** portfolio-management process — the monitoring-and-rebalancing feedback step. No single external standard governs portfolio monitoring.

## Open extensions

- The boundary with BD-07 Investment Risk — SD-05.2 monitors the portfolio as the manager's own oversight; BD-07 is the independent risk function.
- The boundary with SD-10.1 — SD-05.2 monitors drift and risk; SD-10.1 runs the compliance rule engine.
