# SD-16.2 — Owner & Investor Reporting

**Business Domain:** BD-16 Enterprise Governance & Accountability (Cross-cutting — corporate) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Produces the periodic accountability report to whoever the firm answers to — its owners, its beneficiaries, the shareholders of the funds it runs, or its limited partners. SD-16.2 is one capability with many audiences: a sovereign-wealth fund reports to its government, a pension scheme to its trustees and members, a third-party manager to fund boards and shareholders, a private-markets GP to its LPs. The capability is the same — gather the performance, stewardship and accountability data, format it to the audience's standard, distribute it, and handle the queries — only the report template differs. It is **not** the *internal* investment reporting the firm uses to look at itself (that is SD-13.10), and it is **not** the commercial investor-relations and fundraising function — winning investors, the data room, the sales relationship — which belongs to BD-15 Distribution, Product & Client Management (SD-15.13 / SD-15.14). SD-16.2 is *accountability* reporting; it answers to those the firm is accountable to.

## Service Operations

- **Produce the owner / beneficiary report** — the periodic accountability report to a government owner, a board, or a scheme's trustees and members.
- **Produce the investor / LP report** — the capital-account statement, the fund-shareholder report and the periodic LP report, to the relevant disclosure standard.
- **Handle accountability queries** — receive and answer the questions owners, beneficiaries and investors raise on the report.
- **Run the owner / investor meeting** — the mechanics of the annual general meeting, the LP meeting and the owner accountability session.
- **Govern the reporting package** — control the content, accuracy and assurance of the accountability report before it is issued.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** internal investment reporting data and dashboards from SD-13.10; the governed metric definitions from SD-13.8; performance from BD-09; the funds' NAV and capital accounts from SD-12.9; stewardship and voting records from SD-12.12; the GIPS-compliant presentations from SD-09.6.
- **Outputs:** the owner, beneficiary, fund-shareholder and LP accountability reports — consumed by the firm's owners, beneficiaries and investors, and the governing bodies.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** the performance, NAV and stewardship outputs across the model; E-01 Legal Entity (the owners and investors); the SD-09.6 GIPS-compliant presentations; the SD-12.12 stewardship and voting records; the SD-12.9 funds' NAV and capital accounts; the SD-13.10 internal investment reporting data and dashboards; the SD-13.8 governed metric definitions.
- **Owns:** the accountability-reporting package — a process artefact.

## Standards

- The **ILPA Reporting Template** (v2.0, January 2025) for LP reporting — its Capital Account Statement section carries the capital-account reporting.
- **GIPS** for the performance presentation (the verified methodology is SD-09.6's).
- The **Santiago Principles** for sovereign-wealth-fund owner reporting; fund-shareholder disclosure regulation (the UCITS KIID / PRIIPs KID, fund factsheets).

## Open extensions

- The audience-template sub-model — owner, beneficiary, fund-shareholder, LP variants.
- The boundary with SD-15.14 Client & Investor Reporting — SD-16.2 is *mandatory accountability* reporting; SD-15.14 is *discretionary, commercial* client reporting.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
