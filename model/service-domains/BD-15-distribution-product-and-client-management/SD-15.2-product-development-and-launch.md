# SD-15.2 — Product Development & Launch

**Business Domain:** BD-15 Distribution, Product & Client Management (Commercial) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Designs, structures, prices and brings a new fund or strategy to market. Where SD-15.1 decides *what* to offer, SD-15.2 *builds and launches* it: it designs the product, selects the legal vehicle and structure, sets the fee and pricing, secures any seed capital, and executes the launch. It is **not** the ongoing governance of the product once live — that is SD-15.3 — and it is not the legal drafting of the fund documents, which is SD-14.9; SD-15.2 owns the product-build decision, SD-14.9 drafts the agreements it needs.

## Service Operations

- **Design the product** — design the strategy, the structure and the terms of the new fund or strategy.
- **Select the vehicle and structure** — choose the legal vehicle, the domicile and the structure the product will use.
- **Set the pricing** — set the fee schedule, the share-class structure and the pricing of the product.
- **Secure seed capital** — arrange the seed or launch capital the product needs.
- **Execute the launch** — coordinate the launch: registration, listing where relevant, and the cross-functional launch readiness.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the product roadmap from SD-15.1; the legal and tax structuring from SD-14.9 and SD-17.4; the fund-registration regimes; market and competitor pricing.
- **Outputs:** the launched fund or strategy — consumed by SD-13.3 Investment Vehicle & Fund Master (the new vehicle's reference data), SD-15.3 (which governs it from launch) and the front office (which manages it).

## Entities

- **Consumes:** PM-01 Fund & Vehicle, PM-10 Fund Terms, PM-05 Legal Vehicle, FO-01 Fund Product (the issued-fund product record the launch populates — SD-15.2 contributes the new product's initial reference record to FO-01, owned by SD-13.3); the SD-14.9 legal and tax structuring; the SD-15.1 product roadmap; the SD-17.4 legal and tax structuring.
- **Owns:** contributes the new product to FO-01 Fund Product (anchored in SD-13.3) and, should a generic **Product** entity be modelled, to it (an open SD-15.3 question); the launched vehicle's reference record is owned by SD-13.3.

## Standards

- The fund-structuring conventions; the **PRIIPs KID** and the UCITS KIID; the fund-registration regimes.
- The EU cross-border distribution of funds framework for the launch registration.

## Open extensions

- The vehicle-and-structure sub-model.
- The launch-readiness checklist.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
