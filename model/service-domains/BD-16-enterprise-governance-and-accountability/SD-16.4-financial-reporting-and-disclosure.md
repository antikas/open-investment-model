# SD-16.4 — Financial Reporting & Disclosure

**Business Domain:** BD-16 Enterprise Governance & Accountability (Cross-cutting — corporate) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Produces the firm's own statutory financial statements and external disclosures — the management company's, or the institution's, annual report and accounts. SD-16.4 is the capability that closes the firm's books and reports its own financial position to the outside world, to the applicable accounting standard, with an audit opinion. It is **not** fund accounting — the *funds'* financial statements and NAV are SD-12.9 — and it is not the regulatory returns, which are SD-16.3. SD-16.4 produces the *firm's* accounts; the firm's books and the funds' books are two separate ledgers with two separate audit opinions.

## Service Operations

- **Run the financial-statement close** — close the firm's general ledger for the reporting period and prepare the statutory financial statements.
- **Prepare the annual report and accounts** — produce the firm's annual report, the financial statements and the directors' and governance disclosures.
- **Prepare external disclosures** — the firm's other mandatory external financial disclosures, to the applicable accounting and disclosure standards.
- **Support the external audit** — provide the audit evidence and manage the external-audit relationship to a clean opinion.
- **Govern the accounting policy** — own the firm's accounting policies and the judgements behind the financial statements.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the firm's general ledger from SD-17.1 Corporate Accounting & Controllership and the management accounts from SD-17.2 Financial Planning & Analysis; the accounting standards; the external auditor's requirements.
- **Outputs:** the firm's statutory financial statements, annual report and external disclosures — consumed by the firm's owners, the regulators, the external auditor and the governing bodies.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** the corporate ledger from SD-17.1; E-01 Legal Entity (the reporting entities); the SD-17.2 firm's general ledger from SD-17.1 Corporate Accounting & Controllership and the management accounts; **E-37 ESG Compliance Result** (from SD-10.9 — the product-level sustainability-compliance results the firm's own sustainability-disclosure record consumes).
- **Owns:** the firm's statutory financial statements — a process artefact.

## Standards

- **IFRS** and the applicable national GAAP for the firm's statutory accounts.
- The audit standards (the **ISAs**) governing the external audit.
- The corporate-disclosure and annual-report requirements of the firm's jurisdiction and listing status.

## Open extensions

- The boundary with SD-12.9 — the firm's accounts versus the funds' accounts.
- The accounting-policy sub-model.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
