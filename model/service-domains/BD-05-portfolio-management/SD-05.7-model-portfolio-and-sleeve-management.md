# SD-05.7 — Model Portfolio & Sleeve Management

**Business Domain:** BD-05 Portfolio Management (Front office) · **Applies:** PUB

## Purpose

Constructs, maintains and delivers model portfolios and strategy sleeves replicated across many accounts — the "construct once, deliver to many" capability. Where SD-05.1 constructs a portfolio, this Service Domain constructs a *model* — a reusable target — and propagates it to a roster of separately managed and unified managed accounts, managing the drift, the per-account customisation and the dispersion that propagation creates. It is the signature capability of the asset manager and the wealth manager; it is dormant for an institution that runs only its own portfolios.

## Service Operations

- **Construct the model portfolio** — build the reusable model or strategist portfolio.
- **Maintain and version the model** — run the ongoing model updates and model rebalancing, with version control.
- **Deliver the model** — publish the model to the platforms and advisers that consume it.
- **Manage sleeves** — manage the discrete strategy and asset-class sleeves within a multi-strategy or unified managed account, and the multi-portfolio optimisation across them.
- **Replicate across accounts** — propagate the model into the account roster, generate the per-account trade lists, and manage account dispersion.
- **Apply per-account customisation** — the tax overlay, restrictions and transitions each account requires.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the model definition; the account roster and each account's constraints and tax lots; the sleeve and unified-managed-account structure.
- **Outputs:** the delivered model, the per-account trade lists, the sleeve allocations and the dispersion reports — feeding BD-06.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Portfolio / Mandate (E-03) — the model and each replicated account; Holding / Position (E-04, `book = ibor`), Instrument / Asset (E-02).
- **Owns:** none — the model portfolio is a Portfolio / Mandate (E-03) of model type.

## Standards

- No external standard governs model-portfolio management. The model-delivery and unified-managed-account patterns are the industry conventions.

## Open extensions

- The boundary with SD-05.1 — SD-05.1 constructs a portfolio; SD-05.7 constructs a model and propagates it to many.
- Whether the model portfolio warrants explicit entity treatment distinct from a portfolio.
