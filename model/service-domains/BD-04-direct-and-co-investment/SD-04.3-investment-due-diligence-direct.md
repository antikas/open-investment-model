# SD-04.3 — Investment Due Diligence (Direct)

**Business Domain:** BD-04 Direct & Co-Investment (Front office) · **Applies:** PRIV

## Purpose

Runs the full diligence on a direct investment that has passed screening — the deep, verify-before-you-commit examination of the asset. Investment Due Diligence is one capability, but its operations branch by mode: diligencing a company, a building, an infrastructure project, a natural-resource asset and a direct loan are genuinely different examinations. The Service Domain is defined as the union of those operation sets, with no mode as the default — it is a coordinated diligence exercise whatever the asset.

## Service Operations

- **Run company due diligence** — the buyout workstreams: commercial and market diligence, financial diligence and quality-of-earnings analysis, legal, tax, operational, management, IT and ESG diligence.
- **Run real-estate due diligence** — title and boundary survey, environmental site assessment, building-condition survey, zoning and planning verification, lease and rent-roll review, property-level underwriting.
- **Run infrastructure technical due diligence** — the independent engineer's assessment of asset condition, construction-risk and demand-forecast review, concession, regulatory and permitting review.
- **Run natural-resource due diligence** — for timberland, farmland and producing energy or mining assets: soil, site and hydrology survey, environmental and biological assessment, harvest / crop / reserve modelling, and assessment of the specialist operator.
- **Underwrite credit** — for a direct loan, the borrower's debt-service capacity, collateral quality, covenant headroom, and downside / recovery analysis.
- **Build the diligence model** — the integrated financial and underwriting model the investment case rests on.
- **Compile the diligence pack** — the per-workstream reports, the risk register and the synthesised findings that feed the investment-committee memorandum.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the screened opportunity and indicative offer (SD-04.2); data-room access, management presentations, third-party advisers and expert networks.
- **Outputs:** the due-diligence pack, the diligence model and the risk register — consumed by **SD-04.4 Deal Structuring** and **SD-04.5 Investment Approval & Authorisation**.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Legal Entity (E-01, the target / borrower), Instrument / Asset (E-02), Direct Real Asset (RA-01), Appraisal (RA-05); the SD-04.2 screened opportunity and indicative offer.
- **Owns:** none — the diligence pack is an analytical artefact.

## Standards

- **Quality-of-earnings** analysis for company diligence; **Phase I / Phase II environmental site assessment** for real estate; the **independent engineer's report** for infrastructure; **harvest / yield and reserve modelling** and the environmental and sustainable-resource assessments for natural resources; the **Four Cs of credit analysis** for direct lending. No single standard spans the modes — the SD's operation set is the union.

## Open extensions

- Whether the diligence operations warrant explicit per-mode operation sub-sets in the third level, or remain a documented union.
- A Direct Investment / deal record entity carrying the diligence pack.
