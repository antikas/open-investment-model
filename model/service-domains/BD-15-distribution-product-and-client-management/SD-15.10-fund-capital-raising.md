# SD-15.10 — Fund Capital Raising

**Business Domain:** BD-15 Distribution, Product & Client Management (Commercial) · **Applies:** PRIV

## Purpose

Runs the GP-side fundraise for a closed-end fund — bringing limited partners into a fund from launch to final close. SD-15.10 is the private-markets fundraising capability: it builds the fundraising materials (the private-placement memorandum, the pitch), runs the data room, manages the LP pipeline, negotiates the commercial terms, and drives the fund to its first and final close. It is the **GP / manager side** of the transaction whose **LP / allocator side is BD-03** — one firm's fundraise is another's fund-commitment decision. It is **not** continuous open-ended distribution — that is SD-15.4 / SD-15.7 — and it is not the fund's investing, which is BD-04.

## Service Operations

- **Prepare the fundraising materials** — produce the private-placement memorandum, the pitch book and the fundraising track record.
- **Run the fundraising data room** — build and run the data room the prospective LPs conduct their diligence in.
- **Manage the LP pipeline** — manage the pipeline of prospective limited partners through the fundraise.
- **Negotiate commercial terms** — negotiate the fund terms and the side letters with prospective LPs (the side-letter *compliance* tracking is SD-10.5).
- **Drive to first and final close** — manage the fundraise to its first close and through to its final close, and the re-ups from existing LPs.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the new fund from SD-15.2; the firm's private-markets track record; the LP universe; the fund terms (PM-10).
- **Outputs:** the closed fund and its LP base — consumed by SD-15.11 (the LPs to onboard), SD-12.8 (the commitments to process), SD-10.5 (the side-letter obligations) and BD-04 (the fund that now invests).

## Entities

- **Consumes:** PM-01 Fund & Vehicle, PM-10 Fund Terms, PM-06 LP Commitment, E-01 Legal Entity (the prospective LPs); the SD-15.2 new fund.
- **Owns:** the fundraise and the LP pipeline — a process artefact.

- The pre-marketing rules of the EU cross-border distribution framework.
