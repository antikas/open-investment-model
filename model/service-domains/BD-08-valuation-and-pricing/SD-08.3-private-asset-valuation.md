# SD-08.3 — Private-Asset Valuation

**Business Domain:** BD-08 Valuation & Pricing (Middle office) · **Applies:** PRIV

## Purpose

Determines and reviews the fair value of holdings in private equity, private credit, real assets and infrastructure — assets with no market price and no daily quote, valued periodically on an estimate of what they would fetch in an orderly transaction. SD-08.3 is the capability for the hardest valuation problem the firm faces: a value that is an opinion, formed quarterly, on an asset that does not trade. For an LP allocator it is largely the **review and challenge of the GP's reported mark**; for a direct investor it is the **production of the mark itself**. SD-08.3 is **not** the modelling of liquid instruments — that is SD-08.2, which builds a value from market data — and it is not the GP-mark monitoring done as part of manager oversight (SD-03.6); SD-08.3 owns the firm's own fair-value conclusion for the private holding.

## Service Operations

- **Run the periodic valuation cycle** — value the private holdings on the quarterly, or other periodic, cycle the asset class works on, on a consistent fair-value basis.
- **Challenge the GP / sponsor mark** — for fund and co-investment holdings, review the general partner's reported valuation, test its assumptions, and challenge marks that look stale, optimistic or inconsistent.
- **Apply the valuation methodologies** — income (discounted cash flow), market (comparable-company and transaction multiples) and cost / recent-transaction approaches, selected by asset and stage.
- **Coordinate third-party valuers** — commission and review independent valuations from external valuers and appraisers — a real-estate appraisal, an infrastructure valuation, a timberland or farmland natural-resource appraisal — and reconcile them to the firm's view.
- **Back-test marks against realised exits** — compare carrying valuations against the prices actually realised on exit, and feed the variance back into the valuation discipline — the IPEV back-testing recommendation.
- **Document the valuation judgement** — record the methodology, the inputs, the comparables and the reasoning, so a Level 3 mark is defensible to auditors and the pricing committee.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** GP capital-account statements and reported valuations, via SD-03 and SD-12.8; portfolio-company and asset financials; market comparables and transaction data; third-party valuation reports; the valuation policy from SD-08.4.
- **Outputs:** reviewed private-asset fair values with their methodology and evidence — recorded as Valuation (E-07), consumed by SD-12.9 Fund Accounting & NAV, BD-09 (private-market performance and PME), BD-07 (private-markets risk) and SD-08.4, the pricing committee.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** E-04 Holding / Position (`book = ibor` for the live holding being valued), E-02 Instrument / Asset, the private-markets entities PM-09 Fund Investment and PM-06 LP Commitment, and E-03 Portfolio / Mandate; the SD-08.4 valuation policy; GP capital-account statements and reported valuations, via SD-03 and SD-12.8.
- **Owns:** **E-07 Valuation** on the **`method = manager_mark`** partition — the manager- or administrator-reported marks of private-markets holdings and directly-held private interests **consumed as an investor in an externally-managed interest** — and the **`method = appraisal`** partition (the real-asset appraisals RA-05 specialises); co-equal with SD-08.1 (`observable_price` / `amortised_cost`) and SD-08.2 (`mark_to_model`). It shares the `manager_mark` value with SD-12.9 Fund Accounting & NAV but is distinguished by producing capability: SD-08.3 records the mark the institution *consumes* as an investor, where SD-12.9 strikes the NAV of a vehicle the institution *operates*. Also owns **RA-05 Asset Appraisal** — the third-party appraisal record (IVS / RICS Red Book) that supports a directly-held real-asset valuation; RA-05 is the audit-trail record behind the E-07 row for a `method = appraisal` valuation applied to an RA-01 holding.

## Standards

- **IPEV Valuation Guidelines** — the International Private Equity and Venture Capital Valuation Guidelines — the standard methodology for private-equity and venture fair value, including the back-testing recommendation.
- **IVS** (International Valuation Standards) and the **RICS Red Book** for real-asset and infrastructure valuation.
- **IFRS 13 / ASC 820** — private assets are predominantly Level 3 (significant unobservable inputs); ASC 820's NAV-as-a-practical-expedient for fund interests.
- The **AIFMD** independent-valuation requirement and **SEC Rule 2a-5**.
