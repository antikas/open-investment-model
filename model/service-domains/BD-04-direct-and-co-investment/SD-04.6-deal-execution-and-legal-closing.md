# SD-04.6 — Deal Execution & Legal Closing

**Business Domain:** BD-04 Direct & Co-Investment (Front office) · **Applies:** PRIV

## Purpose

Completes the signing, funding and legal closing of an approved direct transaction. Execution & Legal Closing turns an investment-committee approval into an owned asset: it finalises the agreements, manages the gap between signing and completion, and runs the funds flow that transfers ownership. It owns the legal completion of the deal — the operational booking of the resulting position is BD-12's.

## Service Operations

- **Finalise the transaction documents** — the sale and purchase agreement, or the credit agreement for a direct loan, with the shareholders' agreement and disclosure letter.
- **Negotiate the risk allocation** — representations and warranties, indemnities, and warranty-and-indemnity insurance.
- **Secure exclusivity and sign** — the exclusivity agreement and the execution of the transaction documents.
- **Manage the signing-to-closing period** — satisfy the conditions precedent: regulatory and antitrust clearance, third-party consents, financing drawdown.
- **Execute the closing** — run the funds flow, complete the transfer of title or shares, and set up escrow for indemnity and post-closing adjustment.
- **Run the completion adjustment** — the completion-accounts or locked-box true-up and the purchase-price adjustment.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the IC approval and conditions (SD-04.5); the deal structure and term sheet (SD-04.4); the financing commitments.
- **Outputs:** the signed and completed transaction, the satisfied conditions-precedent record and the executed funds flow — handed to **SD-04.8 / SD-04.10 / SD-04.11** (post-close) and to **BD-12** (which books the position).

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Legal Entity (E-01), Instrument / Asset (E-02), Transaction (E-05); PM-15 Deal / Investment Opportunity — the authorised deal being closed; closing populates the deal's typed conversion pair to the post-close record — an investment position, a direct loan or a real asset; the SD-04.4 deal structure and term sheet; the SD-04.5 IC approval and conditions.
- **Owns:** none directly — the closed transaction is recorded as a Transaction (E-05), owned by BD-12's operations domains.

## Standards

- The sale-and-purchase-agreement and credit-agreement conventions; signing and closing as distinct events; conditions precedent; warranty-and-indemnity insurance; completion accounts versus locked box.

## Open extensions

- The boundary with BD-12 — SD-04.6 completes the *legal* transaction and runs the closing funds flow; BD-12 *books and settles* the resulting position.
