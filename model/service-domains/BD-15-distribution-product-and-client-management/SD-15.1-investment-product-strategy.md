# SD-15.1 — Investment Product Strategy

**Business Domain:** BD-15 Distribution, Product & Client Management (Commercial) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Decides which strategies and vehicles the firm offers — the product roadmap. SD-15.1 answers *what should the firm sell*: it generates and assesses product ideas, sets the product roadmap, decides whether to build, buy or partner for a capability, and shapes the firm's product range against client demand and the competitive landscape. A product the firm cannot sell is wasted capability; a gap in the range is lost flow. It is **not** the *building* of a product — that is SD-15.2 — and it is not investment strategy, which is BD-01 (the strategy *of a portfolio*); SD-15.1 is the strategy of the *product range*.

## Service Operations

- **Generate and assess product ideas** — source product ideas from client demand, distribution intelligence and the competitive landscape, and assess them.
- **Set the product roadmap** — maintain the firm's product roadmap and the priorities for new strategies and vehicles.
- **Make the build / buy / partner decision** — decide whether to build a capability in-house, acquire it, or partner / sub-advise for it.
- **Shape the product range** — manage the breadth and coherence of the product shelf against client segments and demand.
- **Analyse the competitive and demand position** — track the competitive product landscape and client demand to inform the strategy.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** client demand and distribution intelligence from SD-15.4 / SD-15.7; the competitive landscape; the firm's investment capabilities; the firm's strategy.
- **Outputs:** the product roadmap and the product-strategy decisions — consumed by SD-15.2 Product Development & Launch and the firm's governance.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** PM-01 Fund & Vehicle, E-03 Portfolio / Mandate; the SD-15.4 client demand and distribution intelligence; the SD-15.7 client demand and distribution intelligence.
- **Owns:** the product roadmap — a process artefact.
- **Open question:** a **Product** entity — the firm's offering as an owned, governed record, anchored in SD-15.3 and shared across the product cluster (SD-15.1–15.3).

## Standards

- No single external standard governs product strategy; the consultancy product-strategy frameworks and the Cerulli product-research practice are the references.
