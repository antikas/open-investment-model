# SD-15.11 — Client & Investor Onboarding

**Business Domain:** BD-15 Distribution, Product & Client Management (Commercial) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Onboards a new client or investor — turning a won mandate or a closed commitment into a live, operational relationship. SD-15.11 runs the onboarding journey: negotiating and executing the investment-management agreement or the subscription, opening the account, and coordinating the cross-functional onboarding that makes the new client investable. It is **not** the financial-crime KYC and AML check — that is SD-14.3, which SD-15.11 triggers and consumes — and it is not the mandate's *codification* into objectives and constraints, which is SD-01.2; SD-15.11 owns the commercial and operational onboarding, SD-01.2 codifies the mandate it produces.

## Service Operations

- **Negotiate and execute the agreement** — negotiate and execute the investment-management agreement, or process the fund subscription, for the new client or investor.
- **Open the account** — open the client account or investor record and coordinate its operational set-up.
- **Coordinate cross-functional onboarding** — coordinate the onboarding across operations, data, compliance and the front office so the client is investable.
- **Trigger and consume the KYC check** — initiate the financial-crime KYC / AML check (run by SD-14.3) and gate the onboarding on its outcome.
- **Confirm onboarding readiness** — confirm the client or investor is fully onboarded, and hand the relationship to SD-15.13.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the won mandate from SD-15.7, or the closed commitment from SD-15.10; the IMA / subscription documents; the KYC outcome from SD-14.3.
- **Outputs:** the onboarded client or investor and the executed agreement — consumed by SD-01.2 (mandate codification), SD-12.15 Transfer Agency & Investor Dealing (the investor on the register), SD-13.2 (the client as a legal entity) and SD-15.13.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** E-03 Portfolio / Mandate, E-01 Legal Entity (the client / investor), PM-06 LP Commitment, PM-10 Fund Terms; the SD-14.3 KYC outcome; the SD-15.10 closed commitment; the SD-15.7 won mandate.
- **Owns:** the onboarding case and the client-relationship record at inception — a process artefact.
- **Open question:** a **Client / Investor** entity — the client or investor as an owned, governed relationship record, shared across the BD-15 client-management cluster.

## Standards

- The investment-management-agreement conventions; the **ICGN Model Mandate**; the fund-subscription-document conventions.
- AML / KYC at onboarding — the check itself is SD-14.3's (cross-reference).
