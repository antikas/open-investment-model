# SD-15.7 — Institutional Sales & Business Development

**Business Domain:** BD-15 Distribution, Product & Client Management (Commercial) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Runs the firm's sales pipeline — prospecting, pitching and winning the mandates and the capital. SD-15.7 is the front line of the commercial function: it identifies and qualifies prospects, manages the sales pipeline from lead to mandate, runs the pitch and the finals process, and wins the mandate or the allocation. It is **not** the RFP / DDQ response capability — that specialist discipline is SD-15.8 — and it is not the closed-end fundraise, which is SD-15.10; SD-15.7 owns the sales relationship and the pipeline.

## Service Operations

- **Identify and qualify prospects** — source and qualify prospective clients and investors.
- **Manage the sales pipeline** — manage the opportunity pipeline from lead to mandate, with the stages and the forecast.
- **Run the pitch and finals** — prepare and deliver pitches, presentations and the finals process.
- **Win the mandate** — negotiate and close the mandate or allocation, and hand it to onboarding (SD-15.11).
- **Manage the sales relationship** — manage the prospect and consultant relationships through the sales cycle.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the distribution strategy and channel intelligence from SD-15.4; marketing-qualified leads from SD-15.5; the RFP / DDQ responses from SD-15.8; consultant search intelligence from SD-15.9; the product range and the performance track record.
- **Outputs:** won mandates and the sales pipeline — consumed by SD-15.11 Client & Investor Onboarding, SD-15.1 (demand intelligence) and the firm's governance.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** E-01 Legal Entity (prospects), PM-01 Fund & Vehicle, the performance track record; the SD-15.4 distribution strategy and channel intelligence; the SD-15.5 marketing-qualified leads; the SD-15.8 RFP / DDQ responses; the SD-15.9 consultant search intelligence.
- **Owns:** the sales pipeline — a process artefact.
- **Open question:** a **Sales Opportunity** entity — the pipeline opportunity as an owned, staged record.

## Standards

- No single external standard governs the sales process; the pre-marketing rules of the EU cross-border distribution framework constrain prospecting.

## Open extensions

- A **Sales Opportunity** entity.
- The pipeline-stage and forecast sub-model.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
