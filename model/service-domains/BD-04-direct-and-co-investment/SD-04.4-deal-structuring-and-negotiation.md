# SD-04.4 — Deal Structuring & Negotiation

**Business Domain:** BD-04 Direct & Co-Investment (Front office) · **Applies:** PRIV

## Purpose

Designs the legal, tax, capital and covenant structure of a direct transaction and negotiates its terms. Structuring is where the diligence findings become a deal: how the asset is owned, how it is financed, how the parties' incentives are aligned, and on what terms. Like diligence, it is one capability whose operations branch by mode — an LBO capital structure, a concession or regulated-asset structure, and a credit covenant package are different structuring problems.

## Service Operations

- **Design the capital structure** — for a buyout, the debt and equity mix, the senior and mezzanine tranching, and the equity cheque.
- **Structure management equity** — rollover equity, the management incentive pool, ratchets, vesting and leaver provisions.
- **Design real-asset and project structures** — concession, public-private-partnership and build-operate-transfer structures, regulated-asset-base models, project-finance special-purpose vehicles and joint ventures; and, for natural resources, operating-lease, crop-share and specialist-operator (TIMO / farmland-operator) mandate structures.
- **Structure the credit** — for a direct loan, covenant design, the security and collateral package, and loan tranching (first-lien, second-lien, unitranche).
- **Design the tax and acquisition-vehicle structure** — the holding-vehicle structure and jurisdiction.
- **Negotiate the term sheet** — price, structure, governance and covenants; and, where applicable, form the club or consortium and negotiate the co-bidder agreement.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the diligence pack and model (SD-04.3); investment-committee guidance; lender appetite.
- **Outputs:** the capital or financing structure, the management-equity plan, the acquisition vehicle, and the negotiated term sheet — consumed by **SD-04.5 Investment Approval & Authorisation** and **SD-04.6 Deal Execution & Legal Closing**.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Legal Entity (E-01), Instrument / Asset (E-02), Direct Real Asset (RA-01); the SD-04.3 diligence pack and model.
- **Owns:** none — the structure and term sheet are analytical and contractual artefacts.

## Standards

- The LBO capital-structure conventions; concession / public-private-partnership and regulated-asset-base structures; credit-agreement covenant and security conventions. No single standard spans the modes — the SD's operation set is the union.

## Open extensions

- The interaction with SD-04.6 — structuring designs the deal; SD-04.6 executes the documents that enact it.
