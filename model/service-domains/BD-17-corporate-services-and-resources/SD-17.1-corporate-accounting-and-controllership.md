# SD-17.1 — Corporate Accounting & Controllership

**Business Domain:** BD-17 Corporate Services & Resources (Cross-cutting — corporate) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Keeps the management company's own books and closes its period. SD-17.1 is the firm's record-to-report capability: it runs the corporate general ledger, posts the corporate accounting entries, reconciles the control accounts, and operates the period close that produces a complete, signed-off trial balance. It is the controllership function — the discipline that makes the firm's accounting records correct, complete and auditable. It is the firm keeping its own books, as distinct from keeping its clients'.

It is **not** the production of the statutory financial statements or the external disclosures — that is SD-16.4 Financial Reporting & Disclosure, which consumes this Service Domain's closed ledger. And it is **not** fund accounting — SD-12.9 Fund Accounting & NAV keeps the *operated funds'* books; SD-17.1 keeps the *management company's own*. SD-17.1 keeps the books and closes the period; SD-16.4 turns the closed books into statements.

## Service Operations

- **Maintain the corporate general ledger** — operate the firm's chart of accounts, post the corporate accounting entries, and keep the sub-ledgers (payables, receivables, fixed assets) reconciled to the ledger.
- **Run the period close** — execute the month-end, quarter-end and year-end close: accruals, prepayments, depreciation, intercompany eliminations, and the production of the trial balance.
- **Reconcile and substantiate accounts** — reconcile the bank, control and balance-sheet accounts, and substantiate each balance with supporting evidence for audit.
- **Operate the accounting control framework** — maintain the controllership controls: the segregation of duties, the journal-approval workflow, the close calendar and the audit trail.
- **Support the external audit** — provide the closed ledger, reconciliations and substantiation to the firm's statutory auditors and resolve audit queries.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the firm's revenue (including the management-fee and carry income computed by SD-12.11), the firm's costs and payables, payroll from SD-17.6 Talent & Human Capital, the corporate banking data from SD-17.3, the corporate-tax provisions from SD-17.5.
- **Outputs:** the corporate general ledger, the closed trial balance and the substantiated account balances — consumed by SD-16.4 Financial Reporting & Disclosure, SD-17.2 Financial Planning & Analysis, SD-17.5 Corporate Tax, SD-16.3 Regulatory Reporting & Filings and the governing bodies.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** the firm's fee and carry income from SD-12.11; E-01 Legal Entity (the firm's own entities); the SD-17.3 corporate banking data; the SD-17.5 corporate-tax provisions; the SD-17.6 firm's revenue (including the management-fee and carry income computed by SD-12.11), the firm's costs and payables, payroll.
- **Owns:** the firm's corporate general ledger and closed trial balance — a process artefact.

## Standards

- **IFRS** / national GAAP for the recognition and measurement of the corporate ledger.
- Controllership practice — the close calendar, the journal-approval and account-reconciliation disciplines, the substantiation standard.
