# SD-01.3 — Capital Market Assumptions & House View

**Business Domain:** BD-01 Investment Strategy & Allocation (Front office) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Produces the forward-looking view of return, risk and the macro environment that allocation is built on — the capital-market assumptions (long-horizon expected return, volatility and correlation by asset class) and the house view (the firm's official macro, thematic and asset-class outlook). Where the allocation Service Domains decide what to hold, this Service Domain supplies the expectations those decisions are made against. It is a research-and-views production capability: it produces inputs, it does not run portfolios. The CFA Institute treats capital market expectations as a discipline in its own right, upstream of allocation, and this Service Domain follows that boundary.

## Service Operations

- **Develop long-horizon capital-market assumptions** — expected return, volatility and correlation estimates per asset class over the strategic horizon.
- **Produce the macro house view** — the firm's official forward-looking view on growth, inflation, interest rates and currencies.
- **Conduct thematic and regime research** — the long-horizon structural themes and the market-regime analysis that shape the outlook.
- **Form asset-class outlooks** — the medium-term return and relative-value outlook by asset class.
- **Publish and disseminate the house view** — the research record and its distribution to the investment teams that consume it.
- **Govern the house-view process** — the cadence of review and the triggers that force the view to be revised.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** market and economic data; macro indicators; historical return and risk series; external and sell-side research.
- **Outputs:** a capital-market-assumptions set and a published house view — the long-horizon assumptions consumed by **SD-01.4 Strategic Asset Allocation**, the asset-class outlooks consumed by **SD-01.5 Tactical & Dynamic Asset Allocation**, and the macro view consumed by the selection domains BD-02 to BD-04.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Price & Market Data (E-08), Asset Class (E-09), Benchmark / Index (E-10).
- **Owns:** none. The capital-market-assumptions set and the house view are managed analytical artefacts, not master entities.

## Standards

- No external standard governs capital-market-assumption production. The **CFA Institute** treats capital market expectations as a distinct discipline upstream of strategic asset allocation; the major asset managers publish long-horizon capital-market-assumption sets that serve as a de-facto reference.

## Open extensions

- Whether a versioned capital-market-assumptions artefact warrants a reference structure, so an allocation can be traced to the assumptions it was built on.
- The boundary with SD-09.5 Investment Analytics & Insight, which produces forward-looking analytics of a different kind.
