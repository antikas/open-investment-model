# SD-15.4 — Distribution Strategy & Channel Management

**Business Domain:** BD-15 Distribution, Product & Client Management (Commercial) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Defines the firm's distribution model and manages the channels it reaches the market through. SD-15.4 decides *how* the firm's products get to investors — direct institutional, intermediated and wholesale, platforms and the ETF ecosystem — manages the economics of each channel, and drives geographic and market expansion. It is **not** the *selling* into a channel — that is SD-15.7 Institutional Sales — and it is not marketing, which is SD-15.5; SD-15.4 sets the channel strategy, the others execute within it.

## Service Operations

- **Set the distribution strategy** — define the firm's distribution model and the channels it will use, by client segment and geography.
- **Manage the channels** — manage the firm's distribution channels — direct, intermediated / wholesale, platforms, the ETF ecosystem — and the relationships behind them.
- **Manage channel economics** — manage the economics of each channel: the distribution fees, the platform terms, the rebates.
- **Drive market and geographic expansion** — assess and execute expansion into new markets and geographies, including the cross-border fund-distribution registration.
- **Analyse distribution performance** — track flows, redemptions and net sales by channel, product and geography.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the product range from SD-15.3; client-segment and demand data; the cross-border distribution framework; flow and net-sales data.
- **Outputs:** the distribution strategy, the channel model and the distribution analytics — consumed by SD-15.5, SD-15.7, SD-15.1 (demand intelligence into product strategy) and the firm's governance.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** PM-01 Fund & Vehicle, E-01 Legal Entity (the distributors and platforms); the SD-15.3 product range.
- **Owns:** **E-25 Account** (`account_type = register` partition) — the fund-register / nominee account held by an intermediary or distributor at the transfer agent; SD-15.4 is the authoritative source for the register partition of E-25, co-equal with SD-12.5 (safekeeping) and SD-11.7 (cash). **FO-09 Omnibus Account** (specialises E-25 Account, register partition) — the registered account relationship between the fund and each distributor, platform or sub-transfer agent that holds units in omnibus, including the servicing fee terms (12b-1 / sub-TA / revenue-share / platform fee) the manager pays for investor servicing within the omnibus. Promotes the distribution-channel account relationship from a process artefact to a first-class data record.

## Standards

- The EU **cross-border distribution of funds** framework and the pre-marketing rules.
- The **UCITS / AIFMD** distribution-passport regimes.

## Open extensions

- The channel sub-model — direct, intermediated, platform, ETF.
- The cross-border registration sub-model.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
