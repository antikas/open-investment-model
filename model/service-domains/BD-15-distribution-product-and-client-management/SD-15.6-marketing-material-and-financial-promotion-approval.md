# SD-15.6 — Marketing-Material & Financial-Promotion Approval

**Business Domain:** BD-15 Distribution, Product & Client Management (Commercial) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Reviews and approves all client-facing promotional material before it is issued — the operational pre-issue control that keeps the firm's marketing fair, clear and not misleading, and compliant with the financial-promotion regime. SD-15.6 is a control capability: every piece of marketing material, every financial promotion, every pitch document passes through its review and sign-off. It exists because a financial promotion is regulated speech — the SEC Marketing Rule and the FCA financial-promotion rules hold the firm answerable for every claim and every performance figure. It is **not** the *production* of the material — that is SD-15.5 and SD-15.7. It is **not** investment compliance, BD-10 — BD-10 monitors the *portfolio*, SD-15.6 monitors the *outgoing communications*. And it is **not** the firm's CCO function — that is **SD-14.2 Corporate Compliance & Conduct**, which *sets* the conduct-compliance standard SD-15.6 *applies* on every specific item. The Marketing Review Committee (or equivalent body) — typically compliance-staffed but operationally embedded in the distribution flow — is SD-15.6's; the CCO and the regulator-facing compliance programme are SD-14.2's. SD-15.6 sits in BD-15 because the capability runs *operationally* inside the distribution workflow.

## Service Operations

- **Review promotional material** — review every client-facing marketing and promotional item against the financial-promotion rules.
- **Approve and sign off** — approve, reject or require changes to promotional material, and record the sign-off.
- **Govern performance and claims presentation** — check that performance, track-record and comparative claims are fair, balanced and compliant (the SEC Marketing Rule's performance-advertising requirements).
- **Maintain the promotion register** — maintain the register of approved promotions and their review trail.
- **Manage the approval standards** — maintain the firm's standards and templates for compliant promotional material.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** draft marketing and promotional material from SD-15.5, SD-15.7 and SD-15.8; the financial-promotion regulation; performance data from BD-09.
- **Outputs:** approved promotional material and the promotion register — consumed by SD-15.5 / SD-15.7 (cleared to issue) and SD-14.2 / SD-14.8 (the compliance and audit evidence).

## Entities

- **Consumes:** the marketing material and the performance records; the SD-15.5 draft marketing and promotional material; the SD-15.7 draft marketing and promotional material; the SD-15.8 draft marketing and promotional material.
- **Owns:** the promotion-approval register — a process artefact.

## Standards

- The **SEC Marketing Rule** (Rule 206(4)-1) — including the performance-advertising requirements.
- The **FCA financial-promotion** rules (COBS 4).
- The UCITS / PRIIPs marketing-communication requirements and the cross-border pre-marketing rules.
