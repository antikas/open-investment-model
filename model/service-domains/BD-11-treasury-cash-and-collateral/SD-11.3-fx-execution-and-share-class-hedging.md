# SD-11.3 — FX Execution & Share-Class Hedging

**Business Domain:** BD-11 Treasury, Cash & Collateral (Middle office) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Executes the currency transactions the funds and portfolios need, and runs the operational share-class-hedging programme. SD-11.3 is the treasury *execution* layer for FX: it converts currency for trade settlement and income repatriation, and it runs the rules-driven hedging of currency-hedged share classes — mechanically hedging a hedged share class's currency exposure back to the share-class currency each NAV cycle. It is **not** the currency-*overlay decision* — deciding a portfolio's net currency exposure and hedge ratio is a portfolio-construction capability, BD-05's SD-05.4 Overlay & Hedging Management, executed through BD-06; SD-11.3 executes the FX the front office and the share-class structure require, it does not decide the exposure.

## Service Operations

- **Execute settlement FX** — convert currency to settle trades that cross currencies, on the spot and forward markets.
- **Execute income and cash-flow FX** — convert income, distributions and other cash flows into the required currency.
- **Run the share-class-hedging programme** — calculate and execute the FX hedges that keep a currency-hedged share class hedged to its share-class currency, each NAV cycle.
- **Manage hedge roll and rebalancing** — roll the forward hedges as they mature and rebalance them as the hedged position changes.
- **Net FX exposure for execution** — net offsetting currency requirements across the funds before executing, to reduce cost.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** settlement and income cash flows requiring conversion; the share-class structure and hedge ratios from SD-13.3; overlay-hedging instructions from SD-05.4; FX market data from SD-13.4.
- **Outputs:** executed FX transactions and share-class hedge positions — consumed by SD-12.4 Trade Settlement, SD-12.9 Fund Accounting & NAV (the hedged share-class NAV), and SD-12.1 IBOR.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** E-06 Cash Flow Event, E-08 Price & Market Data (FX rates), E-03 Portfolio / Mandate; the SD-05.4 overlay-hedging instructions; the SD-13.3 share-class structure and hedge ratios; the SD-13.4 FX market data.
- **Owns:** none directly — the FX transactions are recorded as E-05 Transaction; the share-class hedge positions are E-04 Holding / Position.

## Standards

- The share-class-hedging conventions of the fund-administration practice (the openfunds share-class taxonomy).
- **ISDA** documentation for the forward FX the hedges use; ISO 20022 / SWIFT for FX confirmation and settlement.

## Open extensions

- The share-class-hedging sub-model — hedge ratio, roll, rebalancing tolerance.
- The boundary with SD-05.4 — SD-05.4 decides the overlay, SD-11.3 executes the FX.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
