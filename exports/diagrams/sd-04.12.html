SD-04.12 — Loan Monitoring & Workout PRIV

Business Domain: BD-04 — Direct & Co-Investment · Office: Front

Purpose

Manages a directly-originated private loan through its life — the post-close discipline of direct lending. Direct private credit runs the BD-04 deal chain like any other direct investment — originated, underwritten, structured, approved and closed — but its post-close phase is neither company stewardship nor real-asset operations: it is credit management. This Service Domain owns it — monitoring the borrower against the loan's covenants, processing the changes the loan needs over its life, and working out the positions that deteriorate. It is the third of BD-04's post-close disciplines.

Service Operations (6)

  • Monitor covenant compliance
  • Monitor borrower performance
  • Process amendments and waivers
  • Run amend-and-extend
  • Manage credit deterioration
  • Execute workout and restructuring
