# SD-14.9 — Legal & Contract Management

**Business Domain:** BD-14 Enterprise Risk, Control & Assurance (Cross-cutting — corporate) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Manages the firm's legal agreements across the investment lifecycle, and provides the legal function the firm runs on. SD-14.9 owns the contracts — drafting and reviewing them, holding them in a governed repository, tracking the obligations they carry, and managing disputes — and provides the firm's legal advice and legal-risk management. The agreements span the whole model: the limited-partnership agreement, the investment-management agreement, side letters, NDAs, the ISDA Master Agreement and Credit Support Annex, clearing agreements, leases, service contracts. It is **not** the *negotiation* of an investment's commercial terms — that is the front office's (SD-04.6 closes a deal) — and it is not contractual *compliance* monitoring — SD-10.5 tracks side-letter obligations against the portfolio; SD-14.9 owns the agreement as a legal document.

## Service Operations

- **Draft and review agreements** — draft, review and negotiate the firm's legal agreements across the investment lifecycle.
- **Maintain the legal-document repository** — hold the executed agreements in a governed, retrievable repository.
- **Track contractual obligations** — register and track the obligations the firm's agreements carry, and their key dates.
- **Manage disputes and litigation** — manage legal disputes, litigation and the associated legal risk.
- **Provide legal advice** — provide the firm's legal advice and legal-risk assessment across its activities.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the agreements requiring drafting or review from across the firm; the executed agreements; legal and regulatory developments.
- **Outputs:** the executed agreements, the legal-document repository and the obligation register — consumed by every Business Domain that relies on a legal agreement, and SD-13.11 Document & Content Management (the repository of record).

## Entities

- **Consumes:** E-01 Legal Entity (the counterparties to the agreements); **RA-03 Lease / Tenancy** — legal custody of the executed lease as a document, and the contractual-obligation register over it (RA-01 holds the asset record, RA-02 the operating record, RA-03 the lease-as-data entity; SD-04.10 sets and maintains the lease's economic content — rent, reviews, breaks — while SD-14.9 holds the executed legal document and tracks its obligations).
- **Owns:** the legal agreements as documents — **DR-03 Master Agreement**, **DR-05 Clearing Relationship**; the source of **PM-10 Fund Terms** (the LPA is the authoritative document). The legal-document repository is shared with SD-13.11 Document & Content Management.

## Standards

- The **ISDA** documentation architecture (the Master Agreement, the CSA, the definitions booklets).
- The **ILPA** model legal documents for the LPA and side letters; standard market agreements (the GMRA, the GMSLA).
- Contract-law and the firm's jurisdictions' legal requirements.

## Open extensions

- A Legal Agreement / Contract entity generalising the LPA, IMA, ISDA and lease.
- The obligation-tracking sub-model.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
