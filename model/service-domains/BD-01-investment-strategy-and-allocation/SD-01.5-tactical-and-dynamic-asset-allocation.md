# SD-01.5 — Tactical & Dynamic Asset Allocation

**Business Domain:** BD-01 Investment Strategy & Allocation (Front office) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Manages medium-term deviations from the strategic allocation — the shorter-horizon, signal-driven adjustments made within the policy bands to exploit relative-value views or respond to the market regime. Tactical asset allocation acts on short-horizon expectations; dynamic asset allocation acts on a longer, regime-aware set of expectations. Both are the ongoing counterpart to the periodic strategic decision: SD-01.4 sets the baseline allocation, and this Service Domain deviates from it under delegated discretion. The split from SD-01.4 is one of time horizon and governance, not asset class.

## Service Operations

- **Monitor relative value across asset classes** — valuation and signal monitoring of each asset class against its history and fair value.
- **Form tactical and dynamic views** — the medium-term thesis for deviating from the strategic weights, on short-horizon signals or regime-aware expectations.
- **Size tactical tilts within policy bands** — set the over- or under-weight against each asset class, bounded by the SD-01.4 policy bands.
- **Direct tilt implementation** — hand the sized tilt to portfolio construction and overlay management (BD-05) for physical or synthetic expression.
- **Monitor and review tactical positions** — track each tilt against its thesis and its band, and exit when the thesis is invalidated or the horizon is reached.
- **Govern the tactical mandate** — the delegated discretion limits and approval levels under which tactical deviation operates.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the strategic allocation and policy bands (SD-01.4); the house view and asset-class outlooks (SD-01.3); market data.
- **Outputs:** sized tactical and dynamic tilts — handed to **BD-05 Portfolio Management** for implementation (SD-05.4 Overlay & Hedging Management expresses them physically or synthetically) and monitored against thesis and band.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** Asset Class (E-09), Price & Market Data (E-08), Portfolio / Mandate (E-03), Benchmark / Index (E-10); the SD-01.3 house view and asset-class outlooks; the SD-01.4 strategic allocation and policy bands.
- **Owns:** the tactical-tilt set as an analytical artefact.

## Standards

- The **CFA Institute** treatment of tactical and dynamic asset allocation — the distinction between the two being the horizon of the expectations input, not a different activity. No single external standard governs tactical signal generation.

## Open extensions

- The boundary with SD-05.4 Overlay & Hedging Management — where the tilt decision ends and the overlay implementation begins.
