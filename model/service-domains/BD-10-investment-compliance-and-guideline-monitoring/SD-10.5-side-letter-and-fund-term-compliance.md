# SD-10.5 — Side-Letter & Fund-Term Compliance

**Business Domain:** BD-10 Investment Compliance & Guideline Monitoring (Middle office) · **Applies:** PRIV

## Purpose

Tracks and enforces the negotiated obligations a fund owes its investors under side letters and fund terms — the most-favoured-nation elections, the excuse and exclusion rights, the bespoke reporting and notification clauses, the fee and co-investment provisions an LP negotiated outside the main fund agreement. SD-10.5 exists because a private-markets fund does not have one set of terms — it has the limited-partnership agreement plus a stack of side letters, each granting a particular investor something specific, and those promises must be tracked and kept. It is the compliance capability for the *contractual* obligations a fund carries to its investors, distinct from the *regulatory* and *mandate* compliance the rest of BD-10 handles.

## Service Operations

- **Maintain the side-letter obligation registry** — record every side-letter and fund-term obligation, the investor it is owed to, and what it requires.
- **Process MFN elections** — run the most-favoured-nation process: disclose the side-letter terms to electing investors and apply the elections.
- **Enforce excuse and exclusion rights** — apply an investor's right to be excused from, or excluded from, a particular investment when the trigger arises.
- **Track bespoke reporting and notification obligations** — ensure the specific reporting, notice and consent clauses individual investors negotiated are met on time.
- **Confirm side-letter compliance** — produce the evidence that the fund's contractual obligations to its investors are being honoured.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the executed side letters and the LPA / fund terms (PM-10 Fund Terms); the investor register; the fund's investment pipeline from SD-04.5 for excuse / exclusion triggers.
- **Outputs:** the obligation registry, MFN-election outcomes and side-letter-compliance evidence — consumed by SD-12.8 Capital Call & Distribution Processing (excuse / exclusion affects the funds flow), SD-16.2 Owner & Investor Reporting, and BD-14.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** PM-10 Fund Terms, E-01 Legal Entity (the investors), PM-06 LP Commitment; the SD-04.5 fund's investment pipeline.
- **Owns:** the side-letter obligation registry — currently a process artefact over PM-10 Fund Terms.
- **Open question:** whether a **Side-Letter Obligation** entity is warranted — each obligation a recorded, owned, trackable item with the investor it is owed to and its trigger.

## Standards

- **ILPA** guidance on side letters and most-favoured-nation elections.
- The limited-partnership agreement as the governing contract; the ILPA Reporting Template for the reporting obligations side letters often vary.

## Open extensions

- A **Side-Letter Obligation** entity.
- The MFN-election sub-model.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
