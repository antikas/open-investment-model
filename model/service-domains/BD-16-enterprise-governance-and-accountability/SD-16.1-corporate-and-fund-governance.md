# SD-16.1 — Corporate & Fund Governance

**Business Domain:** BD-16 Enterprise Governance & Accountability (Cross-cutting — corporate) · **Applies:** BOTH

## Purpose

Operates the firm's governance bodies — and supports the governing bodies of the funds the firm runs. SD-16.1 is the capability that makes the firm's decision structures work: it runs the board and the committees, prepares their packs, keeps the calendar, takes the minutes, records the decisions, and maintains the governance framework — the terms of reference, the delegations, the committee structure. It also supports the governing bodies of the *vehicles* the firm manages — a fund board, a UCITS ManCo board, a set of independent directors, a limited-partner advisory committee. It is **not** the *investment* decision a committee makes — that is the front office's (SD-04.5 runs the investment-committee decision; SD-01.x sets the mandate) — SD-16.1 operates the committee *as a governance body*, it does not take the investment call.

## Service Operations

- **Operate the board and committees** — run the firm's board and its committees (audit, risk, remuneration, nomination): the calendar, the secretariat, the agenda.
- **Prepare governance packs and record decisions** — produce the board and committee packs, take the minutes, and record the decisions and the actions arising.
- **Maintain the governance framework** — the committee structure, the terms of reference, the schedule of matters reserved, and the delegations of authority.
- **Support fund governance bodies** — provide secretariat and governance support to the boards of the funds the firm runs, their independent directors, and the LP advisory committees.
- **Run board-effectiveness and governance review** — periodic board-effectiveness reviews and the maintenance of the firm's governance against the applicable code.

## Inputs and outputs

- **Inputs:** the matters requiring governance decision from across the firm; risk, compliance, audit, performance and finance reporting; the applicable governance code; regulatory governance requirements.
- **Outputs:** governance decisions, minutes, the governance framework and the board-effectiveness findings — consumed by every Business Domain (decisions cascade), the regulator and the firm's owners.

## Entities

- **Consumes:** the governance inputs from across the model; E-01 Legal Entity (the firm and the fund vehicles); **FO-08 Service-Provider Appointment** (reads the `role = trustee`, `role = depositary` and `role = auditor` appointment rows for each fund — the governing bodies that BD-16 supports require visibility of the independent appointments the fund has in place; the trustee, the depositary and the auditor are the three independent parties whose appointment is a fund-governance matter, confirmed from FO-08 for each managed vehicle).
- **Owns:** the firm's governance framework and decision record — currently a process artefact. Whether a Governance Body / Committee entity is warranted is an open question.

## Standards

- The **UK Corporate Governance Code** (listed firms) and equivalent corporate-governance codes.
- The **AIC Code of Corporate Governance** for investment companies; **ICGN** Global Governance Principles; the **Santiago Principles** for sovereign wealth funds.
- Fund-governance regulation — the UCITS ManCo and AIFM governance requirements; the role of independent fund directors.

## Open extensions

- A Governance Body / Committee entity.
- The fund-governance-support sub-model — the firm's own bodies versus the funds' bodies.
- The Service-Operation-level input/output contracts.
